The Grindstone

Read by the author, Robert Frost

The poem was published in New Hampshire, which in 1924 won Frost the first of four Pulitzer Prize awards for poetry.

"To produce a thing of value, man must sacrifice his own natural desires, subuit to suffering, and impose a cruelly tyrranical standard upon himself."
